CIPO

Cipo is the mascot of ITS Civil Expo, which has existed since 2011. Various philosophies adorn Cipo. Cipo's shape and color symbolize the concrete material, which means that civil engineering students have a strong mentality in dealing with pressure like concrete resistant to compression.

Cipo is also characterized as wearing a project helmet PPE as a characteristic of civil engineering later when working in the field, while the yellow hue on the helmet indicates the primary color of the ITS HMS flag as a reference for the movement of the ITS Civil Expo .
